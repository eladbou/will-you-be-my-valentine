# How to Deploy Your Website (GitHub Pages)

Since your code is already on GitHub, follow these 4 steps to make your site live:

1.  **Open Your Repository**
    Go to this link: [https://github.com/eladbou/will-you-be-my-valentine](https://github.com/eladbou/will-you-be-my-valentine)

2.  **Go to Settings**
    Click on the **Settings** tab at the top of the repository page (far right).

3.  **Enable GitHub Pages**
    - On the left sidebar, scroll down to the "Code and automation" section.
    - Click on **Pages**.
    - Under **"Build and deployment"**, find the **"Branch"** dropdown.
    - Select **`main`** branch and ensure the folder is **`/(root)`**.
    - Click **Save**.

4.  **Get Your Link!**
    - Use the refresh button after a minute or two.
    - You will see a green box with: **"Your site is live at..."**
    - Copy that link and send it to her! ❤️
